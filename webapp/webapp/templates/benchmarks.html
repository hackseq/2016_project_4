Benchmarking Methodology

In order to evaluate our methods, we created a benchmark dataset using high confidence variant calls in GM12878 reported by Genome in a Bottle (Zook,..., Salit (2014) Nature Biotech). We compared methods by calculating overall precision and recall. Aware that genomics assays survey specific regions of the genome, we also separated our benchmark by genetic context (CDS, UTR, Introns, etc) and distance from transcription start site (TSS) and evaluated performance on these subsets.
